About me

Angel Cervantes is a student who grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area. She graduated high school in 2020 and started her studies at the University of British Columbia that same year. After realizing her love of web developement, she took off a year of school to focus on it, but plans to return to college in the fall of 2023.

In addition to coding, she loves to dance. She danced Hawaiian Hula Dance from the ages 4 to 18. Starting at the age of 8, she participated in numerous Hula competitions alongside her cohorts winning on multiple occasions. Another style of dancing that she’s also studied under is hip hop. She started in high school as a sophomore, but later joined in a local group called “Krazy8”, a hip hop competition group, in her senior year. She moved into “Str8jacket”, the adult version of “Krazy8”, a year later. She has been dancing with them since returning back to the Bay Area.
