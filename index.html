About me

Angel Cervantes is a student who grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area. She graduated high school in 2020 and started her studies at the University of British Columbia that same year. After realizing her love of web developement, she took off a year of school to focus on it, but plans to return to college in the fall of 2023.

In addition to coding, she loves to dance. She danced Hawaiian Hula Dance from the ages 4 to 18. Starting at 8 years old, she participated in Hula competitions until where her groups placed and won multiple times. Another type of dance that she does is Hip Hop. She started in high school as a sophomore, but joined Krazy8, a hip hop competition group, in her senior year. She moved into Str8jacket, the adult version of Krazy8, a year later. She has been dancing with them whenever she has been back from college.
